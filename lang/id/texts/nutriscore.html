Apa itu Nutri-Score?

Nutri-Score adalah sebuah logo yang menunjukkan kualitas nutrisi produk - produk makanan yang diwakili oleh nilai A sampai E. Dengan Nutri-Score, produk dapat dibandingkan dengan mudah dan cepat.

Open Food Facts mulai menghitung Nutri-Score pada tahun 2014 pada aplikasi seluler dan situs web perintisnya, ketika masih berupa makalah penelitian, dan telah mengiringi perkembangannya dari "skor 5 warna" menjadi "Nutri-Score" yang Anda lihat di supermarket saat ini. However, the Nutri-Score is not affixed to all products and Open Food Facts allows you to have this information by scanning the product. Public Health France has supported Open Food Facts since 2018 to facilitate consumer information. For more on the history of Open Food Facts and the Nutri-Score, you can read our dedicated page.

Bagaimana cara Nutri-Score dihitung?

The Nutri-Score is calculated from data in the nutrition facts table which appears on the back of the packaging and ingredients (fiber, fruits and vegetables, dried vegetables).

It is determined by the amount of healthy and unhealthy nutrients:

Poin negatif: energi, lemak jenuh, gula, natrium (kadar yang tinggi dianggap tidak sehat)

Positive points: the proportion of fruits, vegetables and nuts, of olive, colza and nut oils, of fibers and proteins.

Bagaimana saya dapat melihat Nutri-Score produk - produk makanan?

Nutri-Score is a voluntary labeling system. It appears on the label of food products only in some countries and for brands which commited to the score, but it is computed for all products of all countries on Open Food Facts since 2014!

Anda dapat melihat Nutri-Score produk - produk makanan dengan aplikasi seluler iPhone dan Android Open Food Facts, dan di situs web Open Food Facts. Jika produknya belum ada di Open Food Facts, Anda dapat menambahkannya dalam waktu satu menit dengan menambahkan informasi nilai gizi dan memilih kategori, dan Nutri-Score akan dihitung dan ditampilkan dengan segera.

Siapa yang membuat Nutri-Score?

Nutri-Score telah diusulkan oleh EREN, sebuah tim peneliti nutrisi publik Perancis, yang dipimpin oleh Profesor Serge Hercberg. It is based on the nutrition score created by the Food Standards Agency in the UK.

The principle of simplified labeling on the front of packaging was officially presented for the first time in the 2013 report "Proposals for a new impetus to the French public health nutrition policy in the framework of the National Health Strategy" (pdf). In it, Professor Serge Hercberg advocates for the adoption of logo with a system of grades from A to E that would be displayed on the front of food products to allow to easily compare the nutritional quality of products.

Negara mana yang telah menerapkan Nutri-Score?

The Nutri-Score has been adopted by French public authorities in 2017, and then in Belgium, Switzerland, Germany, Luxembourg and Spain. Nutri-Score bersifat opsional di negara - negara ini, tetapi semakin banyak produsen makanan menampilkannya di produk - produk mereka:

  • Merek dengan Nutri-Score di Perancis
  • Merek dengan Nutri-Score di Belgia
  • Merek dengan Nutri-Score di Spanyol
  • Brands with Nutri-Score in Germany
  • Brands with Nutri-Score in Luxembourg
  • Brands with Nutri-Score in Switzerland

Time peneliti di negara lain, seperti Meksiko, juga melakukan penelitian berbasis Nutri-Score.
