Changamoto ya Google.org Athari kwa Hali ya Hewa - Kupima athari za mazingira kwa chakula

Ukweli wa Chakula wazi umechaguliwa kama mmoja wa wafadhili wa 2020 ya Google juu ya changamoto ya Hali ya Hewa, iliyoandaliwa kwa ushirikiano na mkono wa hisani wa’ Google.org na Jumuiya ya Ulaya ya maarifa ya hali ya hewa ya’, Hali ya Hewa-KIC. 

Changamoto yetu: uchunguzi wa athari za mazingira kwa bidhaa zote za chakula huko Uropa

Ujumbe wazi wa Chakula’ uwezo wa watu kuchagua chakula bora kwao na kwa sayari, kwa kuunda uwazi kamili wa mfumo wa chakula.

Unlike nutrition, it’s hard to assess the exact impact of all your personal food choices on the planet and change your behavior accordingly.

To counter this problem, we will compute environmental footprints for millions of food products across Europe. As for the Nutri-Score, we link them to barcode scanning so that consumers and producers can take specific actions to reduce their impact.

Tunatumaini kuwa itasaidia kufufua mazingira, na endelevu zaidi ya kiuchumi kwa kuwezesha wazalishaji kuunda eco-au kuboresha bidhaa wanazouza, na watumiaji kupanga upya uchaguzi wao wa chakula kwa sayari.

Pia tunamatumaini ya kwamba - kama sisi’tupate mafanikio - kwamba kwa miaka mitano, idadi kubwa ya bidhaa za chakula kuuzwa katika Ulaya itakuwa na athari ya mazingira mbele na juu ya maandishi ya pakiti kutambuliwa na mamia ya mamilioni ya walaji, na wazalishaji itakuwa inapunguza athari zao.

Changamoto ya Athari ya Google.org ni nini?


Kwa maneno ya Google.org’, “Programu hii imeundwa kusaidia kuongeza mipango iliyopo ili kukidhi changamoto zinazohitajika za kushughulikia mabadiliko ya hali ya hewa. Inakubali €10M kufadhili maoni ya ujasiri ambayo yanalenga kutumia teknolojia kuharakisha uropa ’kuelekea mustakabali wa kijani kibichi zaidi hapo mbeleni.”

Kwa maneno mengine (yetu), hii ni ruzuku kubwa (euro milioni 1,1) kwa kazi kubwa tuliopendekeza kwa mashindano ya Google Impact Challenge na kuharakisha kazi sisi’tumekuwa tukifanya kwa mazingira.

Tushaanza na alama ya Eco... lakini ni mwanzo tu!

Chini ya miezi 2 baada ya kuanzisha Eco-Score huko Ufaransa, tayari tumeona watumiaji na wazalishaji wanajihusisha na Eco-Score na kuanza kubadilisha tabia zao. That’s great!

Tathmini za mazingira zinapaswa kukubaliwa na kila nchi, na kwa kila bidhaa. Kwa mfano, kulingana na njia za usafirishaji, njia za ufugaji na kilimo, au asili ya viungo, athari za mazingira zitatofautiana.

Ujumbe tunazotumia pia ni Kifaransa sana. Tuna habari nyingi kuhusu utofauti maarufu wa jibini la Ufaransa, lakini ujembe kidogo sana kuhus utaalam wa Uropa kama waffle ya Ubelgiji au Bigos ya Kipolishi. Kupanua kwa nchi zote za Ulaya kutahitaji rasilimali nyingi zaidi kuliko uzinduzi wa kwanza wa Eco-Score, ambayo ilileta kazi nzuri na ADEME.

Tunataka kuwapa wazalishaji hesabu sahihi sana kuwasaidia kubadilisha bidhaa zao kuwa bora zaidi na hivyo itaongeza athari zetu zaidi kupita tabia ya watumiaji hadi mabadiliko ya kimfumo.

Mabadiliko haya makubwa ya kiwango ndio Changamoto ya Google Impact inahusu. Uzalishaji yanayohusiana na chakula inawakilisha sehemu kubwa ya hewa ya carbon duniani, na tunahisi kazi mazingira sisi’tunayofanya inaweza kuwa sehemu ya majibu.

Kupata tathmini ya mazingira kwa kiwango kinachofuata

Kisha tutaongeza mfumo mpya kwa Ukweli wa Chakula, ambayo itatuwezesha kuhesabu haswa athari ya mazingira ya bidhaa za chakula na kurekebisha zaidi hesabu za Eco-Score kwa nchi zingine, pamoja na kushughulikia kesi ambazo tunakosa data, au kwamba tunahitaji kutathmini aina mpya ya bidhaa (kama waffle ya Ubelgiji, Bigos Kipolishi au Turron ya Uhispania).

Helpful tools for consumers

We will ensure you can assess the environmental impact of your food in the Open Food Facts apps, and get useful information about it.

Adding environment support to our free platform for producers

Tunataka kuwapa wazalishaji hesabu sahihi sana kuwasaidia kubadilisha bidhaa zao kuwa bora zaidi na hivyo itaongeza athari zetu zaidi kupita tabia ya watumiaji hadi mabadiliko ya kimfumo.

Support the rapid growth of the Eco-Score both on the digital and physical world

Support the rapid growth of the Eco-Score both on the digital and physical world through our apps, open API ecosystem and new and existing partnerships (producers, governments, researchers, apps…)

In parallel, we will:

  • Adapt our producer platform and apps to help gather even more data about the environmental characteristics of products.
  • Gather additional environmental assessments to support global expansion
  • Support the rapid growth of the Eco-Score both on the digital and physical world through our apps, ecosystem and new and existing partnerships (producers, governments, researchers, apps…)

What it will change, and what it will not change

Please rest assured that this grant won't change anything about the Open Food Facts projects and goals. 

We will of course continue to release everything that we do for free as open source and open data (The power of open source and open data was actually noted as a strong point for maximizing impact in our application).

Google.org is financially helping us (and other winners) accelerate a complex technological project to expand our environmental impact to all Europe. The condition is we actually execute the project we proposed, and help make a large impact on the carbon challenges we all face.
